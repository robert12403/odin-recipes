Tacos

In the following recipe you will learn to make the tacos you are seeing in this image.

Authentic beef tacos are either sliced seared steaks, or marinated beef chopped into thin strips that are pan fried to get golden crispy edges.

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp oil
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 1 onion
  • 500g beef
  • 2 tbsp tomato paste

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a large skillet over high heat. Add garlic and onion - cook 2 min until golden
  2. Add beef and cook 2 min
  3. Add seasonings and cook 2 mins
  4. Add tomato paste and water and cook for 1 minute
