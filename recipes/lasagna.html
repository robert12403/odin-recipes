Lasagna

This is an image of the delicious lasagna that you can make using the following recipe.

Lasagna is a traditional italian recipe. Traditionally it uses cow meat, which is probably called beef in English, maybe. But this is not my language so who knows! Anyway a mix of cow and pork is also accepted. Also, it is common for the Lasagna sauce to be prepared one day before.

Ingredients

  • Cheese Filling
  • Meat
  • Sauce
  • (optional) Spinach

Steps

  1. Boil pasta in a large pot of salted water
  2. Prepare meat sauce: cook sausage and beef with onion and garlic. Drain well, add the pasta sauce and simmer it for a few minutes to thicken.
  3. Combine cheese mixture: Stir the cheese mixture together in a bowl
  4. Layer & bake: Layer the meat sauce and cheese mixture with lasagna noodles and bake until the top of the lasagna is golden brown.
  5. Let the lasagna rest for at least 15 minutes.
